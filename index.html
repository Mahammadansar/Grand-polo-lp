Featured Developer

Grand Polo Club & Resort

Grand Polo Club & Resort is more than just a residential community; it’s a lifestyle destination designed for comfort, leisure, and convenience. With vast green landscapes, state-of-the-art equestrian facilities, and various retail, dining, and entertainment options, residents enjoy a seamless blend of luxury and nature.

A 5.54 million sqm equestrian-inspired masterplan featuring polo fields, lush greens, elegant architecture, and 22 bespoke villa clusters designed for serenity, community, and luxury living.

Located just minutes from Al Maktoum Airport, situated at the northern intersection of Expo Road (E77) and Emirates Road (E611), the resort ensures exceptional accessibility to Dubai's key destinations, and surrounded by future-ready infrastructure, this heritage-rich community redefines upscale family living.

About Grand Polo Club & Resort

Where is the Grand Polo Club & Resort located?

5 min from Al Maktoum Airport; 20 min to Dubai Hills Mall; 28 min to Dubai Marina Mall; 35 min to Downtown Dubai & Dubai Mall.

What types of villas are available?
